This advanced stock evaluation model combines multiple proven investment factors to identify high-quality, undervalued companies with strong growth potential and financial stability. The strategy is designed for long-term investors, with a minimum holding period of one year.

Key Formula:

Final Score = Composite Score * (1 - (Risk Score * 0.5))

Where:
Composite Score = (w1 * Value Rank) + (w2 * Quality Rank) + (w3 * Growth Rank) + (w4 * Financial Health Rank)
Risk Score = (Beta Rank + Debt-to-Equity Rank) / 2

Weights (w1, w2, w3, w4) adjust based on market conditions:

Bull market: 25% Value, 20% Quality, 30% Growth, 25% Financial Health
Bear market: 30% Value, 25% Quality, 20% Growth, 25% Financial Health
Core Metrics:

Value: Acquirer's Multiple (EV/EBIT)
Quality: Return on Invested Capital (ROIC) and Earnings Stability
Growth: 3-year Revenue Growth Rate
Financial Health: Altman Z-Score and Debt-to-Equity Ratio
Additional Features:

Percentile ranking used for all metrics to normalize scores
Quality Rank combines ROIC and Earnings Stability
Financial Health Rank combines Z-Score and Debt-to-Equity ratio
Liquidity filters: Minimum market cap and average trading volume
Exclusion of stocks with Beta > 2
(What is beta: a measure of volatility relative to the overall market, such as the S&P 500, where a value greater than 1 is more volatile than the market)
Rationale:

Balanced perspective on company performance and potential
Emphasis on financial stability and sustainable growth
Risk-adjusted scoring to enhance long-term returns
Flexibility to adapt to changing market conditions
This model offers a data-driven, holistic approach to stock selection, combining the strengths of various investment philosophies while mitigating individual weaknesses. The formula provides a quantitative method to identify companies with strong fundamentals, reasonable valuations, and the potential for sustained long-term performance.

핵심 공식:
최종 점수 = 종합 점수 * (1 - (위험 점수 * 0.5))
여기서:
종합 점수 = (w1 * 가치 순위) + (w2 * 품질 순위) + (w3 * 성장 순위) + (w4 * 재무 건전성 순위)
위험 점수 = (베타 순위 + 부채 비율 순위) / 2
가중치 (w1, w2, w3, w4)는 시장 상황에 따라 조정됩니다.
강세장: 가치 25%, 품질 20%, 성장 30%, 재무 건전성 25%
약세장: 가치 30%, 품질 25%, 성장 20%, 재무 건전성 25%
핵심 지표:
가치: 인수자 배수(EV/EBIT)
품질: 투하 자본 수익률(ROIC) 및 이익 안정성
성장: 3년 매출 성장률
재무 건전성: Altman Z-Score 및 부채 비율
추가 기능:
점수 정규화를 위해 모든 지표에 백분위수 순위 사용
품질 순위는 ROIC와 이익 안정성을 결합합니다.
재무 건전성 순위는 Z-Score와 부채 비율을 결합합니다.
유동성 필터: 최소 시가총액 및 평균 거래량
베타가 2보다 큰 주식 제외
(베타란: S&P 500과 같은 전체 시장 대비 변동성 지표로, 1보다 크면 시장보다 변동성이 큼)
근거:
기업 성과 및 잠재력에 대한 균형 잡힌 관점
